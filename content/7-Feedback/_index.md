### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment was friendly and open. The FCJ members were always willing to help whenever I encountered difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace was clean and comfortable, allowing me to stay focused and work efficiently.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
My mentor provided detailed guidance, explained concepts clearly whenever I had questions, and always encouraged me to ask for clarification. The admin team also offered excellent support with documentation and administrative tasks, ensuring that everything ran smoothly. I especially appreciated that my mentor encouraged me to solve problems independently instead of simply giving me the answers.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The tasks assigned to me were closely related to what I had learned at university while also introducing me to new technologies and concepts that I had not encountered before. This allowed me to strengthen my foundational knowledge while gaining valuable practical experience.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
Throughout the internship, I developed many new skills, including using project management tools, collaborating effectively within a team, and communicating professionally in a workplace environment. My mentor also shared valuable real-world experiences that helped me better understand my future career path.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture was very positive. Everyone respected one another and worked professionally while maintaining a friendly atmosphere. Whenever urgent projects arose, team members collaborated and supported each other regardless of their roles. This made me feel like a valued member of the team, even as an intern.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company provided an internship allowance and offered flexible working arrangements when necessary. In addition, having the opportunity to participate in internal training sessions was a significant benefit that enhanced my learning experience.
